My name is Wallace Sales Gomes, and I am a passionate Full Stack developer dedicated to software development. My journey into computing began in high school, where I took my first computer courses.

In 2023, my military service came to an end, and since then I have continued working in software development, focusing on projects using Java and PHP with Laravel and Angular. Additionally, I have been committed to continuous software improvement, leveraging Sonar as a key tool.
